Frostfire Mine overview

Frostfire Mine is a timed event (commonly 30 minutes) where the goal is to gather Orichalcum by occupying different veins. Several guides note that troops in Frostfire Mine are not permanently killed, so you can play aggressively. Advanced teleports may be available with cooldowns, letting you reposition quickly.

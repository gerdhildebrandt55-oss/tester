FAQ

  • Is WOSC an official Whiteout Survival server?
    No. WOSC is a community-run Discord built by players for strategy and coordination.
  • What is SVS in Whiteout Survival?
    SVS (State vs State / State of Power) matches two states and rewards points across phases like matchmaking, preparation and battle.
  • How often does Bear Hunt / Bear Trap happen?
    Bear Hunt is an alliance event that can be activated roughly every two days and lasts about 30 minutes.
  • Can I move to another state?
    Yes, via the State Transfer event, but there are restrictions (state age difference, hero generation, and more).
  • What is Frostfire Mine?
    A 30-minute event where you gather Orichalcum by occupying veins; troops are not permanently lost inside the mine.

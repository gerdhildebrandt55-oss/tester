Bear Trap / Bear Hunt basics

Bear Hunt is an alliance event that can be activated by alliance leadership and typically runs for about 30 minutes. Many guides describe it as repeatable roughly every two days. Rewards scale with damage, so coordination matters.

How to maximize damage (simple rules)

  • Strongest marches closest to the trap / best positioning for top hitters.
  • Stagger rallies so the bear is taking constant damage instead of downtime.
  • Rally leaders use their best available heroes; rally joiners use buff/support heroes where possible.
